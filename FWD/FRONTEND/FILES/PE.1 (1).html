SPIN LOCKED

Held on December 14, 2024, "Spin Locked" was a strategic and mind-bending event that invited participants to "Break the Whirl, Defy the Loop." Designed to test participants' analytical and problem-solving abilities, this event provided a challenging yet entertaining experience. With lucrative prizes of ₹4000 and ₹2500 for the winners, Spin Locked attracted many eager contestants. Registration was set at ₹100, and the event became a highlight with its competitive spirit and unique mathematical puzzles.

This event was a celebration of math, designed to make the subject accessible and enjoyable to all participants, regardless of their level of expertise. WATT THE F(X)

"Watt the F(x)" was an interactive and playful event held on June 19, 2024, during IEEE PES Week. Organized by Pentagram in collaboration with the IEEE PES club, the event featured creative puzzles and games centered on mathematical functions and problem-solving. With exciting goodies as rewards, the event brought together participants to learn and compete in a lively atmosphere, blending mathematics with fun.

HUNTOPIA

"Huntopia Infinito" was an enthralling treasure hunt event hosted by Pentagram, combining online prelims on December 13, 2023, with offline finals on December 15. With a registration fee of ₹120, the competition challenged participants with a mix of puzzles, riddles, and problem-solving tasks, offering lucrative prizes of ₹5,000 for the winner and ₹3,500 for the runner-up. It served as a platform to test participants' logical and analytical thinking in a fun, competitive environment.

MOCK IPL

"Mock IPL," held on May 17, 2024, was an innovative event that brought together strategy and sportsmanship. Hosted in the BSN Hall, this team-based competition invited participants to bid and strategize as though managing an IPL team. With a registration fee of ₹100 per team and prizes of ₹2,000 and ₹1,000 for the top two teams, it was an exciting event that combined cricket enthusiasm with management skills.
